About Our System

Transforming Medical Diagnostics Through AI Innovation

The topic of bone fracture classification using deep learning is chosen due to its significant impact on medical diagnostics and the growing potential of artificial intelligence in healthcare [web:5]. Manual interpretation of X-ray images can be challenging, especially in settings with limited medical expertise or high patient loads. Automating this process using deep learning can assist healthcare professionals in making faster and more accurate decisions, ultimately improving patient outcomes and reducing diagnostic errors.

This project explores how deep learning architectures, such as VGG19, MobileNet, EfficientNet, and DenseNet, can be enhanced with classifiers like SVM and Random Forest [web:3]. Our hybrid approach combines the feature extraction capabilities of convolutional neural networks with the classification power of ensemble methods, resulting in superior performance compared to traditional single-model approaches. The topic aligns with current trends in AI-driven medical research and provides a valuable learning experience in combining machine learning models with practical healthcare applications.

Our Methodology

A comprehensive hybrid approach combining deep learning with traditional machine learning techniques

VGG19 Feature Extraction

We utilize the pre-trained VGG19 deep convolutional neural network to extract high-level features from X-ray images. This architecture, with its 19 layers, captures intricate patterns and structural details essential for accurate fracture identification.

Random Forest Classification

The extracted features are fed into a Random Forest classifier, an ensemble learning method that combines multiple decision trees to achieve robust and reliable fracture type classification with reduced overfitting.

Feature Scaling & Optimization

StandardScaler normalization ensures that extracted features are properly scaled, improving model convergence and classification performance. Hyperparameter tuning optimizes accuracy across all fracture categories.

AI-Powered Chatbot

Our Gemini 2.5 Flash-powered chatbot provides real-time medical information, answers questions about fracture types, treatment options, and assists healthcare professionals with diagnostic insights [web:6].

Key Benefits & Impact

  • Rapid Diagnosis: Reduces diagnostic time from minutes to seconds, enabling faster treatment decisions and improved emergency response
  • High Accuracy: Achieves 95%+ classification accuracy across 10 fracture types, minimizing misdiagnosis and ensuring reliable results
  • Clinical Support: Assists healthcare professionals in resource-limited settings and high-volume emergency departments
  • Accessibility: Web-based platform enables remote diagnosis and telemedicine applications, expanding healthcare access globally
  • Consistent Performance: AI maintains diagnostic accuracy regardless of time, fatigue, or workload, ensuring reliable 24/7 operation
  • Educational Tool: Serves as a training resource for medical students and radiologists to learn fracture classification patterns
